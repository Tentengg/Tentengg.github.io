Paper Reader

Paper Reader combines PDF import, library folders, structured summaries, research ideas, quick translation, term lookup, paper chat, annotation workflows, vocabulary, Markdown notes, and configurable local storage.

Technical contribution

Engineering areas demonstrated in the project.

  • AI task design: translation, term lookup, summary, ideas, paper chat, and note generation are handled as separate feature tasks.
  • Prompt contracts: prompt files define output formats and rules for consistent feature behavior.
  • Reliability: timeout, retry, backoff, retryable-error detection, and provider fallback are described in the project notes.
  • Output handling: structured outputs such as summaries, mindmaps, and terms are validated and repaired when formatting fails.
  • Grounding: paper chat is designed around saved paper context and uncertainty when evidence is missing.
  • Local data: PDFs, notes, and database storage are organized around a user-configurable local storage root.
